## DESIGN NOTES

**Color Palette:**
- Primary: #050505 (dark background)
- Accent: #CC0000 or #EF4444 (red flag)
- Text: White (#FFFFFF) for headlines, #E2E8F0 for body
- Secondary: #FBBF24 (gold/yellow for highlights, like "Most Popular" badge)

**Typography:**
- Headlines: Syne Bold/Heavy (26–40pt)
- Body: Inter or system font (14–16pt)
- Data/numbers: Monospace (DM Mono, 18–32pt)

**Layout:**
- 16:9 aspect ratio (standard)
- Dark theme (matches brand)
- Whitespace: generous margins, breathing room
- Graphics: use flowcharts, bar charts, trend lines, icons

**Slides with Visuals:**
- Slide 2: Split-screen (problems vs. stats)
- Slide 3: Flowchart (input → scan → output) + screenshot
- Slide 6: Metrics in large, bold typography or icon + number combos
- Slide 7: Bar or curve chart (affiliate growth)
- Slide 8: Timeline (vertical or horizontal)
- Slide 9: Converging arrows or trend lines

**Slide Count:** 12 slides (fits 10–15 min pitch with Q&A)

## NOTES FOR DELIVERY

1. **Opening:** Start with founder story (pain point). Investors invest in founders first.
2. **Problem → Solution:** 1-2 min on problem. 2-3 min on solution. Spend time here.
3. **Traction:** Credibility check. Be honest but frame growth potential.
4. **Roadmap:** Shows you're thinking long-term, not a one-hit wonder.
5. **Ask:** Clear and specific. £50k for 10% is concrete.
6. **Close:** Reinforce vision. "Compliance is the infrastructure for AI."
